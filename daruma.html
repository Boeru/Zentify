七転び八起き (Nana korobi yaoki)

"Fall down 7 times and stand up 8. Never give up!"

STEP 1

Decide on a SPECIFIC GOAL you WANT to ACHIEVE.

STEP 2

DRAW one of Daruma's blank eyes (either eye is OK) to COMMIT yourself to achieve your goal.

STEP 3

Place Daruma somewhere VISIBLE while he focuses on your goal. You'll be reminded to do the same!

STEP 4

Once you've achieved your goal, draw in Daruma's other eye to say THANK YOU!
